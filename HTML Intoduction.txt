Introduction To HTML

	HTML (Hypertext MarkUp Language) is a devised language of computer that makes a website. 
Those websites can be seen or viewed by anyone in this world that are connected in the Internet. 
HTML is easy to learn, with basics which people are accessible. It is also very powerful for what 
it may or can create. It is currently being updated and being versioned. W3C is the one in charge of it.
